The Story

It started with a fascination for movie scores. As a child, I didn't just watch films; I memorized their soundscapes. That early obsession with the relationship between sound and emotion evolved into a lifelong pursuit of the arts.

From age 10, I was performing—playing trumpet, piano, and singing across assorted bands, choirs, and musical theater productions. I learned early on that great art requires both technical discipline and creative freedom.

The Work

Today, I bridge the gap between performance and production. With degrees in Music Recording Technology and Digital Audio, I have formalized that childhood passion into a professional skillset.

My goal is simple: to help you tell your story. Whether you need a crisp podcast mix, a cinematic video edit, or a full-scale audio production, I bring the technical expertise so you can focus on the vision.
