Theatre Production Recording Rates

Flexible pricing for schools, community theaters, and parent boosters.

How Pricing Works (Read First)

1. Flat Rate (Best for Schools/Organizations)

The organization pays a single fee. You receive the master files and full rights to distribute the video to cast, crew, and students for free.

2. Per Copy (Best for Parents/Communities)

No upfront cost to the school. Patrons purchase individual copies directly.
Note: This option requires a minimum number of pre-orders to confirm booking.

Hybrid Option: The school or a parent can pay a base fee upfront to lower the cost per copy for everyone else. Submit a request for a custom quote.
Don't forget your video license: To protect your organization and the show's creators, please remember that video recording rights must be secured separately from your performance rights. It is the hosting organization's responsibility to purchase this authorization from the distributor (e.g., Music Theatre International, Concord Theatricals, Samuel French, etc.) prior to booking. Please reach out to discuss your options!
